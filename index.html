Supporting Our Community

The Aiden Zachary Anderko Community Fridge works with the goal to support the abundance of in-need individuals in the South Carolina community one step at a time with our first location in Chester with the support of Battered but Not Broken Ministries.

Discover the Story

We at the AZA Community Fridge are on a mission to make a positive impact in our South Carolina community through food.

Our goal is to provide access to nutritious and quality food and essential items to those in need, while also promoting others to do the same.

Frequently Asked Questions

Where is the Community Fridge located at?

The current Community Fridge is located in front of Battered but Not Broken Ministries at 564 Old York Road, Chester, SC 29706.

How can I support the Fridge?

We would love for you to support in any way you can, and if you’re unable to now, that’s ok! Items can be brought to the fridge directly, or items can be purchased from our Amazon Wishlist and shipped to us. If you would like to inquire about other ways to support us, please don’t hesitate to reach out!

Support Us

Want to help support our mission through the Community Fridge? Here’s how:

The items described below can be purchased from our Amazon Wishlist and shipped to us for stocking, or you can bring items directly to our site at 564 Old York Road, Chester, SC. Additionally, if you would like to help us financially you can support us at our GoFundMe. If you want to do more, please reach out to find out how you can do so. Any and all help is greatly appreciated in helping our project!
